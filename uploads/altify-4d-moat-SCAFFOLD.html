Where we are · 2D

A flat record is a single loose page.

Whatever fits on the page is all the AI has to work with. So it grabs every other loose page it can find and tries to make sense of the pile. The more it grabs, the cloudier the answer. The seller gets less from more.

This is where the whole market lives today. It is also, honestly, where we live today. Most of our records are not full.

What we already own · 3D

Our record is a page explained by every page beneath it.

One deal, one coherent story: who the people are, the problems, the politics, the process, and whether it can actually win. Now the AI pulls real context. The more it reads, the clearer it gets, because it all hangs together.

Almost no one else has this. It’s the hard thing to build, and it’s ours. The catch: a human still has to fill it, and no one has the hours. So it sits mostly empty.

The build · automating 3D

First we fill it. Without asking anyone to.

The system reads the emails, calls, and notes and fills the deep record on its own. Coverage goes from mostly-empty to complete, with no new work for the seller.

And here’s how we beat the trust problem: anything the AI can’t tie to a hard, checkable fact gets yellow-flagged for a person to approve. Nothing enters the record as a guess. The human keeps the judgment; the machine keeps the record.

The future · 4D

Then it remembers. Forever.

The same system that fills the record also stamps every official change into a long-term memory. Every person, every deal, every account. Each customer’s record now grows across years. The AI sees the whole progression, so the more time passes, the sharper it gets.

This is the compounding loop Nadella described, made real for sales. Every year, every customer gets more from it than the year before. A reason to charge more. A reason they never leave. Say goodbye to Altify, and they say goodbye to the history that wins their deals.

Flat record · 2D

Two years, gone with them.

A seller worked the account for two years. The deals, the people, the friendships. Then they leave. The next rep starts from fog: interrogating the AI, piecing together who’s who, never quite sure what really happened or what anyone cares about.

Living record · 4D

Walk in already knowing everyone.

The new rep opens tomorrow’s call and sees every person who has touched the account over two years. What each cares about, what to watch for, where the politics sit. And the whole arc of the deal. Nothing lost. Nobody starts over.

Four-dimensional selling is the future of enterprise AI. And we’re the only ones holding the hard part.
