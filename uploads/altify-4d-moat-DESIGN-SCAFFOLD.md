# Altify · "The Moat" — Design Scaffold

Internal executive page. Audience: CEO (Pat), then CTO and CFO. Goal: get a yes to build the 4D core. One concept the whole way down — **2D → 3D → 4D** — with Herbert Simon and Satya Nadella as outside voices that confirm the thesis.

This document is a **build scaffold**: every section in order, the exact copy (final, approved), and per-section design direction. Drop it into Design and finish the visuals there. Two visuals need care and are fully specified at the bottom: **the infinity loop** and **the 2D→3D→4D roadmap**.

---

## Global direction

- **Register:** executive copywriting. Short declarative sentences. One idea per line. Concrete nouns. No hedging. Confidence as the default. **No em-dashes in body copy** (periods instead). Em-dash is allowed only in quote attributions ("— Herbert Simon").
- **Tone spine:** we already own the *rare* thing (deep architecture). The *hard* part is ahead. Hard is the point.
- **Palette:** deep navy background (#0F2036), off-white text (#EAF0F6), orange accent (#EA7C31), muted blue-grey (#8AA3BC / #AFC2D6) for secondary text, blue (#53AFD4) for human/AI-human notes. Human-side blue in the loop reads ~#3E9AD6; AI-side red-orange ~#E1673A; collaboration purple ~#7B61C9.
- **Format:** long single-scroll page. Each section is a headline plus at most one or two short paragraphs. Animations optional and additive (curve draws on scroll, roadmap dimensions light up). Must remain legible static / in print for a projector room.
- **Section markers:** small orange eyebrow label above each headline (e.g. "The clock"). Where a dimension is named, show it clearly (2D / 3D / 4D).

# VISUAL A — THE INFINITY LOOP (build exactly this)

**This is the diagram that must come out right.** The failed versions drew two separate ovals plus a few disconnected arrows. That is wrong. It is **one single continuous line** — a figure-8 / infinity path — and the line **changes color as it travels around the loop.**

## The quadrant frame
- Rounded-rectangle plot on the navy background.
- Horizontal axis: **Low Impact** (left) ←→ **High Impact** (right).
- Vertical axis: **Easy for Humans** (top) ←→ **Hard for Humans** (bottom).
- Corner labels (small, muted): "Human + AI Collaboration" in the top-left and bottom-right corners.

## The single continuous loop (one path, gradient stroke)
Picture a figure-8 lying across the two axes, crossing at a node near the origin.

- **Bottom-left lobe = AI WORK.** Low impact, hard for humans. This lobe's stroke is **red / red-orange (#E1673A).** Label inside/near it: **"AI WORK — Administration & Analysis"** with sub-items: Human Data Ingestion, Data Mapping, Contextual Record Analysis, Altify Administration. A soft red glow dot sits in the lobe.
- **Top-right lobe = HUMAN WORK.** High impact, easy for humans. This lobe's stroke is **blue (#3E9AD6).** Label: **"HUMAN WORK — Communication & Strategy"** with sub-items: Emails, Calls & Chats, Notes & Transcripts, Strategic Thought, Other CRM Work. A soft blue glow dot sits in the lobe.
- **The crossover, near the origin,** is where the two lobes meet. There is a small **open circle node** at the crossing.

## The color flow (the key detail)
As the eye follows the **one continuous line** around the figure-8, the stroke color transitions smoothly:

**blue → purple → red → purple → blue → (repeat)**

- The **blue** stretch is the human lobe (top-right).
- As the line comes down and INTO the crossover, blue **blends through purple.**
- Coming out the other side into the AI lobe, purple **blends into red.**
- The **red** stretch is the AI lobe (bottom-left).
- As the line climbs back UP and into the crossover, red **blends through purple** again.
- Exiting the crossover back toward the human lobe, purple **blends back to blue.**

So **purple always marks the hand-off zone** around the crossover — the place where humans and AI meet. Purple is not a separate arrow; it is the transition color of the single line as it passes through the middle. This is what makes it read as one self-perpetuating loop rather than two disconnected shapes.

Implementation note for Design: this is a single `<path>` for the figure-8 with a stroke that follows a gradient along its length (SVG `stroke` with a `linearGradient`/`gradientUnits="userSpaceOnUse"` mapped along the path, or split into 4 arc segments each with its own blue→purple, purple→red, red→purple, purple→blue gradient so the joins are seamless). The important outcome: **continuous line, seamless color shifts, purple at the crossover.**

## Hand-off labels at the crossover
Two small purple labels near the crossing, pointing along the line as it passes through:
- **Reconciliation** (sub: "Headless T&I")
- **Inspections** (sub: "Headless TeamView")

## Closing arc
A short **dotted arc at the top** feeding into the human lobe, labeled **"New tasks & info"** — this shows the loop feeding forward: the output of one pass becomes the input of the next.

## Legend (bottom)
- Red dot — "AI does the grunt work"
- Blue dot — "Humans do what only humans can"
- Purple dot — "They meet: reconcile & inspect"

## Reference
Matthew's uploaded reference image is the target. Match its proportions: two clean lobes of roughly equal visual weight, crossover sitting right on the origin, the human lobe slightly larger and up-and-right, the AI lobe down-and-left. The critical fix vs. prior attempts is the **continuous gradient stroke** (blue→purple→red→purple→blue), not separate ovals with arrows stuck on.

# VISUAL B — THE 2D → 3D → 4D ROADMAP (build exactly this)

Three stages left to right, orange arrows between them.

- **2D** = a single flat rectangular "page" with a few grey text lines on it. Grey stroke. Caption below: **"one flat page."**
- **3D** = an **org-chart hierarchy** (one top box, connector down to a horizontal bus, three child boxes hanging off it). Orange stroke, navy fill. Caption: **"the deal, mapped in depth."** This is the important substitution: an org-chart says *structured depth*, which is what our architecture actually is. Do NOT use a stack of pages here.
- **4D** = the **same org-chart repeated and receding into the distance** (a time-series). Front copy in solid orange; two or three copies behind it, offset up-and-right, progressively fainter (fading to muted blue), implying the same structure captured again and again over time. A small **time arrow** runs left-to-right beneath it. Caption: **"that depth, over time."**

Reference org-chart shape: one parent box on top, a trunk line down to a horizontal bus, three children below. Clean rounded rectangles, thin strokes.
