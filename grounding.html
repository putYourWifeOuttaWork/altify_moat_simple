Today · The existing architecture

Capturing context. Guiding AI.

The scroll showed the 3D record: the deal, mapped in depth. This page opens it up: what the structure actually holds, how its parts read each other, and everything that falls out of that, computed instead of guessed. One deal runs through every drawing: a budget holder nobody has engaged.

Mechanism one · The cross-reference · Inside the maps

Nothing here is a note. Everything is typed.

On the relationship map, every person carries measured attributes: sentiment, political status, buying role, coverage, key player, target. And people reference each other: influence runs from one person to another as its own arrow, and so does conflict. On the insight map, every card is one of five types: a goal, a pressure, an initiative, an obstacle, or a solution, and each is honed by or informs specific people, many to many. Joined, this is the deal's political situation: not a diagram of contacts, a typed graph of who matters, to whom, about what.

Mechanism one · The cross-reference · Across the four

People. Problems. Process. Potential. Each one reads the others.

The maps supply the evidence. The assessment reads it and sets what must become true. The sales process tells everything where we are, and it sequences the assessment: in stage one it would be unfair to flag a seller for lacking influence over the budget holder, you may not even know who that is yet, but budget confirmed, timeline known, need established: required now. And potential is not an opinion. The odds of winning, and the risk of losing, are computed from all of it.

a dozen directed meanings between four aspects. This mesh is the structure AI reads.

Every reference carries its why.

Mechanism two · Traversal

One record ID. Everything hangs off it.

All of the above hangs off a single opportunity record. So AI never searches and never re-reads. It walks the named arrows: opportunity, to people, to the budget holder, to the answer. The answer is a query: a fraction of a second, a fraction of a cent, and identical every time it's asked. Write it once, read it forever.

Retrieval, not re-derivation. Fast, cheap, and it holds still.

Mechanism three · The ontology

The methodology lives inside the record. So AI never guesses.

Each deal selects its own questions by what it is: the solution, the segment, the size. The process sequences them: what is unfair to ask in stage one becomes mandatory by stage three. And every question already knows what must be true for the deal to be winnable, and grades itself from the maps. So guidance is computed, not inferred: what isn't true yet, and, because the maps are right there, exactly who gets you to it. Not "find a budget holder." Three supporters sit in the buying department: they are the path.

Guardrails beget guidance. Not more context.

What the structure buys

Apply AI to this, and intelligence is a query.

Because the structure already holds the meanings, everything downstream is computed, not inferred: roll-up scores, red flags, next best actions, coaching, call prep, forecasting, pipeline analysis. And because it is structured, it grounds more than our own AI. Enrichment tools, calendars, marketing, business development: every system in the ecosystem can read the same truth and write into the same shape.

one structure, every answer downstream, none of it guessed.

One structure. Every answer downstream.

The bridge

Today a seller keeps all of this filled by hand, which is why most records sit empty. Filling it automatically, with a person ratifying anything uncertain, is the rest of the story. And the reason that machine can work at all is the structure on this page.
