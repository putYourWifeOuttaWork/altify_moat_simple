Today · The existing architecture

Capturing context. Guiding AI.

The scroll showed the 3D record: the deal, mapped in depth. This page opens it up: what the structure actually holds, how its parts read each other, and everything that falls out of that, computed instead of guessed. One deal runs through every drawing: a budget holder nobody has engaged.

Mechanism one · The cross-reference

People. Problems. Process. Potential. Each one reads the others.

On the relationship map, every person carries measured attributes: sentiment, political status, buying role, coverage, key player, target. People reference each other: influence and conflict run person to person as their own arrows. On the insight map, every card is one of five types: goal, pressure, initiative, obstacle, solution, each honed by or informing specific people, many to many. And the four aspects read each other: the maps supply the evidence, the assessment sets what must become true, the process sequences what counts right now, and potential is computed, not opined. This is the deal's political situation: a typed graph of who matters, to whom, about what.

Arrow types influence conflict honed by, informs supplies evidence sets what must become true sequences necessitates, informs the stage

Every reference carries its why.

Mechanism two · The ontology

The methodology lives inside the record. So AI never guesses.

Each deal selects its own questions by what it is: solution, segment, size. The process sequences them: what is unfair to ask in stage one becomes mandatory by stage three. Every question grades itself from the maps, so guidance is computed, not inferred: what isn't true yet, and exactly who gets you to it. Not "find a budget holder." Three supporters sit in the buying department: they are the path.

Guardrails beget guidance. Not more context.

Mechanism three · Traversal

One record ID. Everything hangs off it.

All of it hangs off a single opportunity record, so AI never searches and never re-reads. It walks the named arrows, and the answer is a query: a fraction of a second, a fraction of a cent, identical every time. And a query can be asked from anywhere: any assistant, any brief, any app. Write it once, read it forever, from wherever the work happens.

Retrieval, not re-derivation. Fast, cheap, and it holds still.

What the structure buys

Apply AI to this, and intelligence is a query.

Because the structure already holds the meanings, everything downstream is computed, not inferred: roll-up scores, red flags, next best actions, coaching, call prep, forecasting, pipeline analysis. And the same ground carries the whole workflow: enrichment, calendars, marketing, business development, every tool reading the same truth and writing into the same shape.

One structure. Every answer downstream.

The bridge

Today a seller keeps all of this filled by hand, which is why most records sit empty. Filling it automatically, with a person ratifying anything uncertain, is the rest of the story. And the reason that machine can work at all is the structure on this page.
