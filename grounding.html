Capturing context. Guiding AI.

Following is the in-depth explanation of what Altify's structure actually holds, how its parts read each other, and what that means for AI, today.

Mechanism one · The cross-reference

People. Problems. Process. Methodology. Each one reads the others.

Altify's data architecture is the closest thing to the truth of an opportunity or account that CRM has available. People are situated by their own properties and reference each other by reporting structure, operational unit, influence and conflict, while problems carry properties of their own and belong to specific people, to the company, and to the deal, so the two maps interweave concretely. Process and methodology are unique to the deal, enforcing the team's mandated must-haves under the control of the political situation and pointing forward to what must happen next with these particular people and problems. Fully cross-referenced and kept true, this graph gives AI a context without match for quick, concrete, trustworthy retrieval and analysis.

Every reference carries its why.

Mechanism two · The ontology

The methodology lives inside the record. So AI never guesses.

Every deal asks its own questions determined by what solution, segment, size or other conditions set the structure. This is enterprise mandated and cannot be inferred by AI. This is a core differentiator for Altify now and going forward. Our structure is built on and made-up-of methodological first principles. The structure itself determines both where you stand, and where you must go, so by embracing proven processes and methodology with 30 years of heritage, our architecture is purpose-built for enterprise sales AI.

Guardrails beget guidance. Not more context.

Mechanism three · Traversal

One record ID. Everything hangs off it.

The whole situation of a deal or account lives in that record, so instead of searching huge data sets and guessing at meaning, AI walks the path down into the record (traversal) and pulls analysis and guidance from the business meaning written into it (ontology). Retrieval: seconds, a few cents, nearly identical every time. A query can be asked from anywhere: any assistant, any brief, any app. Write it once, read it forever, from wherever the work happens.

Retrieval, not re-derivation. Fast, cheap, and it stays put.

What the structure buys

Apply AI to Altify, and intelligence is simple.

Because the structure already holds the meaning and ground truth, AI doesn't need to guess. The same ground also enables cross-functional workflows like call-preps, enrichment of CRM, and email writing, using applications outside of Salesforce. Every tool reads the same Altify meaning and truth and writes back cohesive, deeper responses.

One structure. Every answer downstream.

The bridge

Today, sellers are trying to keep all of this deep-data filled by hand. Most records sit empty. The Always-On future will allow AI to fill it daily, with people approving any uncertainties.

This bridges the gap from potential AI context and guidance tool to "AI-Ready Data" technology that does what Revenue Action Orchestration companies claim to do, but more concretely and more suitable for enterprise sales teams on Salesforce.
